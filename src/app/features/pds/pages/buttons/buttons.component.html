Material 3 Color System

‚úÖ Correct M3 Color Roles

  • Primary: color="primary" - Main brand color, high emphasis actions
  • Secondary: color="accent" - Supporting brand color, medium emphasis
  • Tertiary: color="accent" - Additional accent, flexible use
  • Error: color="warn" - Destructive/error actions
  • Default: No color attribute - Surface-based, low emphasis
